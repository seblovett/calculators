⚖️ Potential Divider Calculator
Calculate unloaded and loaded output voltage, loading error, and design for a target output — including the effect of a load resistance.

Loaded Potential Divider

Unloaded: Vout = Vin × R2 / (R1 + R2)
Loaded (RL ∥ R2):
R2_eff = R2 × RL / (R2 + RL)
Vout = Vin × R2_eff / (R1 + R2_eff)

Loading error = (Vout_ul − Vout_l) / Vout_ul × 100%
Vout = Vin × (R2 ∥ RL) / [R1 + (R2 ∥ RL)]
⚠ Please enter Vin, R1, and R2.
